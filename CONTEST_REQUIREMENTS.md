# Higgsfield "Make Your Action Scene" Contest

## Key Dates
- **Deadline:** 5 days from Feb 24, 2026
- **Submissions:** 5,772 current entries

## Prize Pool: $500,000
- 1st Place: $150,000 USD
- 2nd Place: $100,000 USD
- 3rd Place: $50,000 USD
- Honorable Mentions (up to 20): $10,000 USD each

---

## Judging Criteria

| Criteria | Weight | What They're Looking For |
|----------|--------|--------------------------|
| **Cinematic Quality** | 25% | Visual polish, camera work, lighting, color grading, overall production value. Does it look like a real action scene? |
| **Storytelling & Creativity** | 25% | Strength of concept, narrative structure, pacing, originality. Does it make you want to see more? |
| **Technical Execution** | 20% | How effectively you used AI generation tools. Quality of asset integration, consistency, purposeful use of technology. |
| **Platform Engagement** | 15% | Views, likes, and clones on your submission within the Higgsfield contest gallery. Community response matters. |
| **Social Media Engagement** | 15% | Reach, views, likes, shares, comments on your public social media post. How well did the video perform in the wild? |

**Total Impact Breakdown:**
- 50% = CRAFT (Cinematic + Storytelling)
- 30% = DISTRIBUTION (Platform + Social)
- 20% = Technical

## Eligibility
1. Must be 18 years of age or older
2. Up to 10 submissions per person
3. Free to enter
4. Contest void where prohibited or in jurisdictions subject to international sanctions

---

## Content Requirements

### Length
- Minimum: 15 seconds
- Maximum: 5 minutes
- **Sweet spot: 30-90 seconds** (optimal for social media)

### Mandatory Elements
1. Project must be in English
2. Must include the official Higgsfield watermark (downloadable from contest page)
3. Must be posted to at least one public social media platform with:
   - Link to Higgsfield
   - **#HiggsfieldAction** hashtag in post/caption

### Allowed Platforms
- Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, X (Twitter), Facebook, LinkedIn
- Post must be public (not private or restricted)

### AI Tools
- **Any model allowed:** Kling, Runway, Pika, Flux, GPT Image, Sora, etc.
- No model restrictions
- Can mix AI-generated and live-action footage
- Can mix multiple AI tools

## Prohibited Content

**INSTANT DISQUALIFICATION:**
1. Copyrighted intellectual property (characters, franchises, brands, logos, music)
2. Pornographic or explicitly sexual content
3. Content depicting, promoting, or glorifying violence against real individuals
4. Political statements, endorsements, or propaganda
5. Religious statements, proselytizing, or disparagement
6. Content that is illegal, defamatory, or violates rights of others
7. NSFW content as defined by Higgsfield's community guidelines

## Multi-Entry Strategy

Since you can submit **up to 10 entries**, you should:
- Test 2-3 different genres/concepts
- Each submission = separate social post
- Learn from early entry performance
- Iterate and improve later submissions
- Diversify your chances across different styles
